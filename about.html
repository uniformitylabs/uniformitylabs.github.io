About Us

home/About

Uniformity Labs’ breakthrough technology helps unlock the full potential of AM

Founded in 2014 and based in Fremont, California, Uniformity Labs (UL) develops breakthrough material and software solutions that aim to accelerate and expand global commercial and industrial 3D printing (“Additive Manufacturing”, “AM”) markets. The company’s patent-pending technology enables significant cost savings, speed & quality improvements across all mainstream AM printers.
